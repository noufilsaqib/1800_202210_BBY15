OUR STORY

In a world full of uncertainty, we have created an app with your safety in mind. Umbrella is for people who like to go traveling whether in pairs/groups and also for party goers who are in need of a more reliable method other than using group chats in keeping track of their friends.

HOW IT WORKS

Create Group

You create a group by entering the meetup point and setting a radius with yourself as the center point. If a member leaves the radius, the app sends the host an alert.

Add Friends

You can add friends to the group by sending them a code which is auto-generated when you create the group. After that, you can sit back and the app will look out for you.

Stay Safe

The host will get an alert in case you start exiting the area without leaving the group. Alternatively, you can also send an alert with just a click of the SOS button.
